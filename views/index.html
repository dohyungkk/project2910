Wise Shopping

"Help Sam with buying food at a grocery store by preventing them from buying too much food. Buying too much food will result in food waste which is a major concern we should avoid doing. If you put back too much food, you will lose and if the timer runs out you will also lose for buying too much food. Match the amount of food on the shopping list to advance to the next level. Play Wise Shopping to help Sam buy food for their family and reduce food waste."
